Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience to join?

Not at all! We welcome students of all skill levels. I'll work with you to build skills from wherever you're starting.

What is the time commitment?

Regular practice sessions are 2-3 times per week, plus additional time during competition season. It's designed to fit with your school schedule.

What equipment do I need?

The school provides computers and software. You just need enthusiasm and willingness to learn!

Can I join mid-season?

Yes, you can join at any time during the school year. I'll help you catch up on what you've missed.
